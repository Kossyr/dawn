12.12.1986 - Stas Shaposhnikov was the first countryman I met after the imprisonment. He was the one responsible for the prisoner exchange and he personally welcomed me back to the USSR. I haven't known him by then - he wasn't under Livanoch back in the day. He was polite enough, although as I knew nothing about him I didn't tell him much.

I spent the next month in Leningrad. More specifically, at the outskirts of the city getting back into shape with some other boys from Spetsnaz. I wasn't slacking in the prison, but I needed more exercise as well as simply holding a gun again. It was a good month. I spoke with Stas more frequently. There were a lot of things we agreed on. Especially regarding the need for change.

The Soviet Union was in a spiral leading to its downfall. Our economy could not keep up with the West. It was a matter of time before we would become irrelevant. There were two options for the future - let it all fall and try to rebuild or strike one hard time at the enemy to break them. Prolonged war was not an option - we would lose. But using nuclear arsenal the enemy could be wiped out before any serious defence is mounted. Killing billions in order to save face and a dying state. This was a despicable act and nobody ever dared to pursue it.Nobody - except for one person.

I got back into shape in Leningrad and grew closer to Stas, who shared my thoughts and agreed with them. After a month, Vladimir Volkov came to see me. It was an interesting meeting. It could have ended either way, I wasn't quite sure if they knew what happened with me and Livanoch. But Volkov was amicable and after a brief exchange, he took me back to the army and made me a high Spetsnaz operative again - although he did not restore me to my previous rank of colonel. I've heard about him from before but seeing him I realized he was very dangerous, perhaps even more than I gave him credit for before. He was very much like Livanoch, ruthless, cunning and desicive. He was a man willing to act upon Livanoch's plan to save the USSR and doom all others.
