6th December 1988 - We got up early this morning. Our goal - reconnaissance. We need to know what the enemy is planning. The easiest is to have a look.

I've seen Lemontov when they woke us up. We only exchanged looks before he disappeared in the morning mist. He's in forward positions deep behind enemy lines. Life of spetsnaz - always exciting.

Today's objective is to scout enemy positions. The two main targets are Malsk in the north and the Dnieper base in the south. The enemy has set up a number of forward camps in the hills so you'll have to fight your way through. Your squad Roman is heading to the northern sector and Malsk, meanwhile squad Konstantin handles the southern sector and the Dnieper base.
Squad Dmitry is in forward position and is also the leader of this operation.

Mission starts at the Don base.

Board the convoy, call sign Leonid. It will take you further down the road.

Make your way through the hills taking care of any enemy patrols you come across.

Scout the enemy positions in Malsk. You have been given binoculars as the squad's designated spotter.

Return back to the waiting convoy which will then return back north.

Retreat back to the Don base on your own.


Mission completed


We know where the enemy is amassing his troops for a strong push north. You did well, comrade, against all odds. Good job!
