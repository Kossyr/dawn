20th December 1987 - Dearest Ekaterina,

we finally turned the tide and it seems today is the final assault. I will be in as an air support, but you should not worry. The enemy has mostly retreated already. It will not be a hard fight.

Comrade commander Syomin is a great man. He is rough but fair. He is also very knowledgable and I learned a lot in the past few days. You know Ippolit, he's still here with me, still trying to get more vodka in me. But I do not need vodka when I have your affection.

I still can't leave, but the ban should be lifted soon - once we win. And victory is close, my love. Then we shall be together and I shall not leave you again.

Forever yours,
Nikolay

The enemy is left with only the southernmost part of the island - where he landed three weeks ago. Now it is high time we drive him from Kolguyev for good.

Your task is to provide air support to our armoured forces in their reclamation of the island.

You start at our heliport in Arzapol.

Board your attack helicopters.

Move south and destroy all enemy forces.

Destroy retreating ships.

Return to the heliport and celebrate.


Mission completed


The battle for Kolguyev is over, the enemy has been vanqushed. For now you are free to celebrate and take some free time, but no leaves are permitted yet.
