5.12.1986 - Day two of me fighting for the Motherland.

The last two days were quite tense - everyone was getting ready for a big offensive which starts today. We were mostly on defense until now and it seems the command is pulling most of our forces to this battle. Our plan is to secure two bases, one in central and one in southern Kolguyev and conquer everything between. It is a bold plan and Yuri is in the centre of it. For Rodina!

Today we are planning on launching a major offensive to advance the front further west. Combined assault of tanks and infantry will secure two bases and a significant amount of territory of central and southern Kolguyev.

Forwards to victory, comrade!

You start with your platoon Yuri near our front line base.

Ride on to the crossroads where you'll be joined by platoon Tatyana.

Continue southwards with Tatyana and clear the road of enemy forces.

Support the attack of squads Anton and Boris against enemy base.

Once the base is secure, head again southwards towards another crossroads. Secure the area and leave platoon Tatyana here. They will cover your flanks.

Ride through the valley up to the southernmost crossroads. Destroy any enemy forces you encounter.

Together with detachment Ivan (tank platoon and three infantry squads), seize the enemy base.

Retreat back to the north!


Mission completed


The offensive was a partial success, but the main target is still held by the enemy. Our position is not favorable and we'll probably have to retreat further north.
