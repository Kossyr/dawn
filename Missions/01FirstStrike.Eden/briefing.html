1st November 1988 - What started as an ordinary morning turned into my squad packing up and boarding a transport helicopter with a one way stop on Everon. All of that in manner of hours. Lieutenant Dostoevsky gathered us on the camp square before the flag, offered little and vague explanation and off we go to war. Eight years of relative peace gone in an instant.

We are close. I can see the shoreline in the distance. My throat is sore and the air turned cold. My first ever combat against real enemy is about to begin.

Comrade soldier, you are part of group Anton. The mission begins on board of a helicopter above the sea.

Your task is to seize the village of Morton. You will approach the village from the west, meanwhile squad Boris will support you from the east.

Seize and hold Morton.


Mission completed


You successfully seized Morton. The first step of conquering Everon was made.
