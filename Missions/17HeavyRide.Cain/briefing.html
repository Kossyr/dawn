5th December 1988 - Day two of my fighting for the Motherland.

My first combat experience and I think I did very well. It is a lot different from training - bad decisions can be fatal here. I am not afraid though - I feel like I am finally where I belong and that makes me confident and resolute. I am not alone in the tank and I am not alone on the battlefield - tank corps is truly a family and I am blessed to be part of the best.

We stabilized the front and are now preparing to strike back. A high ranking officer arrived yesterday to brief comrade Frolov on the plan - I think it was even comrade Zubarev, the commander of our forces. It seems our platoon will be vital in taking back lost territory!

Today we are planning on launching a major offensive to advance the front further west. A combined assault of tanks and infantry will secure two bases and a significant amount of territory of central and southern Kolguyev.

Forward to victory, comrade!

You start with your platoon Yury at the Astrabirsk front lines.

Ride on to the crossroads where you'll be joined by platoon Tatyana.

Continue southwards with Tatyana and clear the road of enemy forces.

Support the attack of squads Anton and Boris against the Yenisei base.

Once the base is secure, head again southwards towards another crossroads. Secure the area and leave platoon Tatyana here. They will cover your flanks.

Ride through the valley up to the southernmost crossroads. Destroy any enemy forces you encounter.

Together with detachment Ivan seize the Neva base.

Retreat back to the north!


Mission completed


The offensive was a partial success, but the main target is still held by the enemy. Our position is not favourable and we'll probably have to retreat further north.
