2.11.1986 - My first battle. I expected a bloodbath, but it was even worse. Bullets flew all around me, soldiers were dying and I kept thinking about one thing - survive. Which I managed.

I still don't fully understand what is going on here. Command didn't bother letting us rest and now we are headed further north. Our goal is to seize Provins, strategically important village. This time we have tank support.

The officer said that if this attack was successful, the conflict is all but decided. I don't know about that, from what I have seen the Americans are much better prepared than we are. We only have about a hundred soldiers on the island and a small number of tanks. It seems there's double the Americans at the very least.

Well, if command thinks we can win just in a few days, I won't stop them.

The war continues. Today's goal is to seize the strategically important village of Provins. Your attack will be supported by tank platoon Fyodor and group Leonid.

You start in a truck en route to the village.

Together with groups Leonid and Fyodor seize Provins

Retreat to Figari.
