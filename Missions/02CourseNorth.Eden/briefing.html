2nd November 1988 - My first battle. I think I did rather well - it was nothing too crazy though. The enemy was not prepared at all and we quickly pushed through their non-existent defences and seized the village. We certainly did surprise them, but that element quickly wears off - it will be harder and harder from now on.

Command didn't bother letting us rest and now we are headed further north. Our goal is to seize Provins, the last village before the city of Montignac. Other squads already took over Figari and we are to finish the push. This time we also have tank support.

The war continues. Today's goal is to seize the strategically important village of Provins. Your attack will be supported by tank platoon Fyodor and group Leonid.

You start in a truck en route to the village.

Get to your designated position by the forest near the enemy lines and wait for the order to attack.

Together with groups Leonid and Fyodor seize Provins.

Retreat to Figari.
