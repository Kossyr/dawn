10th December 1987 - Reinforcements are finally slowly arriving, but the enemy does not sleep. He's going to try one last major attack before we can fully reorganize.

We are defending three fronts - western, central and eastern. Strategic positions are fortified and each incoming road from the south has mines placed on it. I must remember we are outnumbered - so we'll have to be careful. Enemy push in the centre should be the least dangerous but still respectable. Probably few APCs and a lot of infantry.

I've been given command not only of a single squad, but the entire defence of central front. I need to stay focused and not leave designed positions. I should also make sure my squad is geared up before we board the helicopter to the front lines.

The enemy is conducting a major offensive along the entire front to try and make a breakthrough before our reinforcements fully reinforce our army. This must not happen.

You are in charge of the central front. Hold your position against enemy attacks. We are outnumbered - make use of fortifications and the defensive terrain.

You start at the heliport.

Board the helicopter and get to the front lines.

Defend the central front.

Stop the enemy breakthrough in the east.
