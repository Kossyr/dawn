6.12.1986 - Still no message from Ekaterina. I'm starting to think that letters are not allowed out yet. Is the command keeping things here a secret or at least as much shrouded in mystery as possible? What the hell is going on here?

We got up early this morning. Our goal - reconnaissance. We need to know what the enemy is planning. The easiest to have a look.

I've seen Lemontov when they woke us up. We only exchanged looks before he disappeared in the morning mist. He's in forward positions deep behind enemy lines. Life of Spetsnaz - always exciting.

Today's objective is to scout enemy positions. Two main targets are the bases codenamed Tortoise and Deer. The enemy has setup a number of forward camps in the hills so you'll have to fight your way through. Your squad Roman is heading north up to base Deer, meanwhile squad Konstantin handles the south and base Tortoise.
Squad Dmitri is in forward position and is also the leader of this operation.

Mission starts at base.

Board the convoy call sign Leonid. It will take you further down the road.

Make your way through the hills taking care of any enemy patrols you come across.

Scout the enemy base codename Deer. You have been given binoculars as the squad's designated spotter.
