10th February 1989 -

It has been years since our last incursion on Malden and our defeat at the hands of the enemy. Now, we are finally ready to go back and finish this war for good.

The objective of the mission is to neutralize enemy anti-air guns around in the southern Malden so that our air force can conduct a raid on the enemy positions in a few hours. Don't waste your time destroying anything else.

We know of four approximate positions of the enemy guns - we need you to destroy at least three of them.

This is all in preparation for our morning invasion of Malden. You are the vanguard, comrade Lemontov.

You start on the coast where you just landed.

Destroy the anti-air gun around Cancon.

Destroy the anti-air gun around La Riviere.

Destroy the anti-air gun around Sainte Marie.

Destroy the anti-air gun around Le Port.

After successfully completing the objective, hide in the northern hills.


Mission completed
