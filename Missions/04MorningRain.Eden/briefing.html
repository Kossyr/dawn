4.11.1986 - I made a good impression on the officer. He called for me and recommended to the Spetsnaz. I should feel honoured. I guess I am a little. My head is still clouded with doubt and remorse, but at least there is an interesting mission ahead.

As a ten man squad we are to seize Lamentin. Fortunately, I am only in reserve and will probably just guard the road and forest to the east rather than be personally involved in the combat.

Perhaps it is better that way. It will be pretty hot in that city, which I won't have to endure laying in the bushes...

Rashiv, you have been recommended by your commanding officer lieutenant Dostoevsky. I won't ask about your qualities, you shall demonstrate them to me now.

Our starting position is near Lamentin.

Continue with Spetsnaz squad Dimitri to the outskirts of the city. There you will split up, the squad shall go on to position Kalinka, you and Lemontov to position Bear, where he'll leave you.

While Lemontov makes his way to position Dragunov, you will watch the road and forest east of Lamentin. Do not let the enemy surround major Lemontov and provide cover for the squad.

Destroy the APC coming along the northern road.

Listen to major Lemontov and follow his orders.


Mission completed


Good job, comrade. You made sure the operation was smooth. I will personally commend you at comrade Dostoevsky. You can return him now. Good luck.
