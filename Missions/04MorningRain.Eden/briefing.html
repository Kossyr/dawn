4.11.1986 - I made a good impression on the officer. He called for me and recommended to the Spetsnaz. Few hours later and I am on my way to Lamentin.

I am assigned to major Lemontov. Grigory Lemontov - I know that name. He used to be a colonel, but then he also spent the last seven years in American prison. I must confess I did not even know he was back. That man is legendary, his reputation is of ruthless and cunning man. He saw all the battlefields of the world - Afghanistan, Vietnam, Korea, Columbia, South Africa... He is a legend among the troops and for a good reason - he is one of the best soldiers our Motherland can boast.

I should feel honoured. And yet I don't. Lemontov actually reminds of the old times. And those bring back painful memories. No, that's silly. I am silly. I am a soldier and this is my newest mission. Forget all other crap for one night and focus. This is my chance to actually get somewhere.

Let's get to it.

Rashiv, you have been recommended by your commanding officer lieutenant Dostoevsky. I won't ask about your qualities, you shall demonstrate them to me now.

Our goal is to seize Lamentin. We need to encircle the city first and then strike. We have surprise and the morning shadow on our side.

We will join other Spetsnaz before the operation starts. Our first target is the harbour. Squad Ivan supports us from the sea. Next we take control of the roads by seizing checkpoints and the outpost. We will be joined by squad Yelena from the north.

Me and you will then move to the lookout from where we shall coordinate the attack and support it with my long range sniper rifle. You will cover me. The fall of Lamentin will be only a question of time.

We start southeast of the city. Good luck, Rashiv.

Follow comrade major Lemontov along the coast to the harbour. Other Spetsnaz will join you on the way.

Attack and take control of the harbour. Squad Ivan landed on the coast during the night and will support you in the attack.

Seize the enemy checkpoint on the south road. The northern checkpoint will be taken care of by squad Yelena.

Attack and seize the enemy outpost.

Accompany comrade major Lemontov to the lookout point.

Cover comrade major Lemontov during the attack on Lamentin.

Deal with the incoming enemies from the rear!

Seize Lamentin.

Stay close to comrade major Lemontov and follow his orders.
