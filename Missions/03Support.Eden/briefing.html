3.11.1986 - This morning I thought about mother, who I never met, and father, who died in service years ago. I imagined what they would say - my father especially. He always believed in fairness and justice - which are hard to come by in the Soviet Union. He fought the Americans for this very island - before they put a bullet in his brain. Is it justice what we are doing here or revenge?

I try not to attract too much attention. I don't want it. I want... I'm actually not sure what I want. I used to think I wanted revenge on the Americans for what they did to my father, but after experiencing war for myself I do not think it is a course of action I can take anymore. War is bad enough without hatred and disdain on top of it. I do not wish to become an animal craving to kill.

Enough of bad thoughts. We have a new mission - to delay enemy forces until our own guys get to safety. Attack on Le Moule was a complete failure - lucky I was not part of it.

Talking about failures, we seized Provins yesterday - and held it for about two minutes before retreating to Figari, which we also lost after another hour. Now in the north we only hold Morton. With the defeat at Le Moule we are probably just about ready to head home again.

An attack on Le Moule was repelled. Your goal is to support retreating units which are defending themselves at these positions and make sure they retreat back to the farm. You must not allow the Americans to surround our units.

You start en route to Le Moule.

Tank platoon Yuri will unload you at the position of our pursued units. Help them repel any enemy attack and cover their retreat to a farm.

Get yourself back behind our lines.


Mission completed


Lots of soldiers are indebted to you, comrade. You allowed our weakened units to retreat and repelled enemy pursuers. You have done well - know that HQ did notice.
