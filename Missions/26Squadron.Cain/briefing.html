14th December 1987 - Dearest Ekaterina,

I can't believe what I am about to write. I was promoted to attack squadron! Finally, my long held dream is finally coming true! We flew with Ippolit yesterday and were close when an enemy made a breakthrough so we were sent in. We saved an entire squad and destroyed enemy armour - then swept the squad up and got them to the hospital in time. You should have seen those cheers when we got to the barracks!

Although it does pain me at the same time - I will now be on the front lines in the thick of the fighting. It will be dangerous, but this is what I always wanted. I hope you can forgive me for I aim to fulfill my promise - I will return to you the first chance I get. For now, I will fight for our country so that this conflict is resolved as soon as possible. Then we can finally be together.

I'll write to you soon. We're going on our first mission. I know you wish me luck - and I thank you. I always keep the locket with your photo in my cockpit. Now the cockpit does change, but your memento does not. You are still with me up there in the skies.

Forever yours,
Nikolay

Your talent has not gone unnoticed. We are assigning you to comrade colonel Syomin and his attack squadron. Do not disappoint us.

Your mission will be to intercept enemy armoured convoy heading from Arzapol to central Kolguyev along the northern road. It must not reach its destination. Destroy it.

Move south where our units are preparing to retake Astrabirsk. You will provide aerial support and destroy any tank in the vicinity of the village.

You start in your car on way to the heliport.

Get to the heliport and report to comrade Syomin.

Attack and destroy the enemy convoy.

Support ground attack of friendly troops by destroying armoured targets around Astrabirsk.

Return to heliport and land.


Mission completed


Well done, comrade Ablomov. This is exactly what I'm expecting from you. Keep at it and we'll talk about advancement soon enough.
