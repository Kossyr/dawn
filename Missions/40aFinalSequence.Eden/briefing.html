10th February 1989 - Dearest Ekaterina,



Forever yours,
Nikolay

Our spetsnaz commando successfully neutralized enemy air defence in southern Malden. You and your squadron are being tasked with flying to Malden and destroying as many enemy tanks as you can.

The invasion forces are going in right after you. The more you destroy, the easier time they are going to have.

Be careful to the north of the island, as the enemy air defence there is fully intact. South is the only raid's objective. Good luck, comrades!

You start at the airport.

Board your Su-25 and follow your squadron to the runway.

Take off from the Everon airport.

Head north-west towards Malden. Destroy all enemy armoured vehicles in the southern part of the island.
