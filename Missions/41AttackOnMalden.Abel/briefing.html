10th February 1989 -

Rejoice, comrade, for the final victory is near!

We are invading Malden. After our spetsnaz softened their defences and our jets destroyed their armoured vehicles, it is time for the infantry to secure the southern part of the island, concluding the first step in our way to win this war.

You command the eastern paratrooper groups and your target is Chapoi. All the details are in the objectives' description.

There has been a development - one of our jets crashed in the middle of the enemy territory. Your drop point is closest - take a detour and make haste to locate the crash site and find the pilot before the enemy does. Return to the mission afterwards.

You start in the air with the rest of our invasion forces approaching Malden. Good luck!

Helicopters will take you over to the drop zone east where you will paradrop. Groups under the command of Anton are paradropping the the drop zone west. They have their own mission objective with Anton itself assigned to protect your west flank. You are supported by groups Leonid Black who will help you in your attack on Chapoi, Leonid Red who supports the eastern landings and Leonid Green who supports the attack on Le Port. The latter two groups also guard the roads to prevent reinforcements from reaching Chapoi.

Before moving towards Chapoi, investigate the crash site and find the pilot.

Together with your supporting groups, attack and seize the town of Chapoi.

Hold the city until our landing forces reach you.


Mission completed


A grand victory, comrades, the enemy has been soundly beaten. We are ready for the final assault that will end this war.
