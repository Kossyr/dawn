Everon. It's been a while. But I am back with Lemontov with a seemingly simple mission - talk to the rebels and get them to join us in the upcoming attack. It's easier said than done.

They don't like us. We probably don't really like them either. But we do share a common enemy. There is just a slight problem - similar situation happened once before. We faced the same enemy back then - we supplied them with weapons, equipment, training. They were about to defeat the Americans before we showed up and smashed them to bits.

Old grudges die hard. We betrayed them and some of those French still remember it. It will be hard to look past that and cooperate on anything. Even with their lives at stake they might refuse us and then there's nothing I can do for them anymore.

I vowed to help them. I vowed to come back. Now I am here, but what a mere man like me can do? Can I make sure the bloodshed ends? That we all get to return home and forget the squabble between us? What can I do but try my hardest? Will it be enough though?

If I falter now, the rebels are doomed. The orders are clear. They are with us or we will destroy them. Again.
