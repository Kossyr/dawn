9.11.1986 - Notes

Plan

Board the jeep waiting in the castle courtyard.

Infiltrate the base in Saint Pierre disguised as American soldiers.

Refuel the boats, otherwise you cannot escape from Everon.

Ask around for corporal Calverra and get him to refuel the boats.

Calverra wants you to get the fuel truck from Durras. Ask for Tracer.

Tracer won't give you the fuel. You'll have to steal it.

Return with the fuel truck to Calverra.

Get your hands on some explosives.

The explosives are guarded. Distract the guards and take the explosives. Try to look around.

Meet up with Gaston at the cemetery.

Create a distraction to decrease the number of guards watching the boats.

Steal one of boats in the port and escape from Everon.


Mission completed


Finally, I escaped from Everon. It will take several hours to reach Kolguyev, but I am finally on my way. I need to bring the news of an invasion to my brothers - although I leave Everon behind, the war seems to be following still.
