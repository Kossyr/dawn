28.11.1986 - It's been three weeks since Everon. We have fortified Kolguyev and are waiting for the enemy attack which will surely come.

I told what I've learned to captain Shaposhnikov and he shared my doubts though even he didn't know who came up with that rebel story - who is behind our attack on Everon. The one responsible for this whole mess. I'll give him time, but I fear it's someone very much high up - and digging into it could cost me my life.

The last few days were spent mostly on patrol, day and night. The attack still has not come, but we all know it's close. It is only a matter of time.

I had a lot of time to think. The people on Everon are valiant and they desire peace, although they had to go to war to achieve it. I feel like I went through a purgatory on that island. I feel somehow different. I am no longer a shy boy lurking in the shadows, fearful of old memories haunting him.

I see a new path ahead of me. An old chapter is closed and a fresh one is beginning. I embrace it.
